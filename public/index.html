FAQ
Got Questions?
Everything you need to know about learning with UpliftEdu.
Do I need prior experience to enroll?▼

No! Most of our courses are beginner-friendly and start from the fundamentals. Whether you're a fresh graduate, a working professional looking to switch careers, or someone re-entering the workforce — our structured curriculum meets you where you are.

How are classes conducted?▼

We use a hybrid model combining live interactive sessions (1-2 per week, 1-1.5 hours each, max 30 students per batch) with recorded content you can access anytime. Live sessions allow real-time Q&A with instructors, while recordings give you flexibility to learn at your own pace.

What certifications will I receive?▼

Upon completion, you receive an Illinois Tech Professional Certificate and NSDC-recognized credentials. Select courses also include additional certifications (e.g., PwC Tax Professional Certificate for Accounting, AWS certification for cloud courses). You also get an internship certificate upon capstone project completion.

How does placement assistance work?▼

Our dedicated career support team provides: resume building & review, LinkedIn profile optimization, mock interview sessions, soft skills training, and direct connections to our 500+ hiring partner network including companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Deloitte, Accenture, and EY. While we don't guarantee placement, our structured support significantly boosts your chances.

What is the refund policy?▼

We offer a 30-day refund window. Before batch commencement, a 20% processing fee is deducted. After commencement, 40% is deducted. You can also defer to another batch once at no extra charge within 30 days of starting. We want you to feel confident about your investment.

Are EMI options available?▼

Yes! We offer zero-interest EMI options to make education accessible. Pay in easy monthly installments without any additional charges. Contact our counselling team at 9380058580 for personalized payment plans.

How long do I have access to course materials?▼

You get 1 year of access to all recorded sessions and course materials from the date of purchase. This gives you plenty of time to complete the course, revise, and reference materials even after you've landed your new role.
