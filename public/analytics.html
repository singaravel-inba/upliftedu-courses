📖 KPI Dictionary — 15 Core Metrics

Click any metric to expand its full definition, formula, source, and rationale.

💰 MRR

₹18.4L
Formula
Sum of all payments received in calendar month
Source
Razorpay settlements
Frequency
Daily auto-update
Owner
Founder + Finance
Why
Core revenue health. Declining MRR for 2+ months = systemic problem.

📊 CAC

₹2,840
Formula
(Total marketing spend + sales salaries) ÷ New enrollments
Source
Meta/Google Ads + Payroll → Razorpay
Frequency
Weekly (rolling 30d)
Owner
Marketing + Founder
Why
Efficiency of spend. >₹5K = investigate channel ROI. Target: 10-15% of ARPU.

🔄 LTV:CAC

8.7x
Formula
Avg Revenue/Student ÷ CAC
Source
Razorpay + Ad platforms
Frequency
Monthly
Owner
Founder
Why
>5x excellent. <3x = overspending or underearning per student.

📞 Leads/Day

23
Formula
New lead form submissions per calendar day
Source
Website popup + Ads → CRM
Frequency
Real-time
Owner
Marketing
Why
Top-of-funnel health. <10/day for 2+ days = pipeline broken.

🎯 Lead→Paid %

11.2%
Formula
(Enrolled ÷ Total leads) × 100, rolling 30 days
Source
CRM + Razorpay
Frequency
Daily
Owner
Sales Head
Why
Sales efficiency. Industry avg 8-10%. <8% = lead or counseling quality issue.

👥 Active Learners

612
Formula
Students with ≥1 session/assignment in last 14 days
Source
LMS logs
Frequency
Daily
Owner
Operations
Why

📅 Attendance %

78%
Formula
(Sessions attended ÷ Sessions scheduled) × 100
Source

Owner

Why

🏆 Placement Rate

93%
Formula
(Placed within 90 days ÷ Graduates seeking placement) × 100
Source
Placement tracker
Frequency
Weekly per batch
Owner
Placement + Founder
Why
THE outcome metric. Drives word-of-mouth and marketing claims. <85% = investigate.

😊 NPS

72
Formula
% Promoters (9-10) minus % Detractors (0-6)
Source
Google Forms survey
Frequency
Per batch, monthly aggregate
Owner
Ops + Founder
Why
>50 excellent, >70 world-class, <40 red flag. Predicts referral rate.

↩️ Refund Rate

2.1%
Formula
(Refunds ÷ Enrollments) × 100, rolling 30d
Source
Razorpay refund logs
Frequency
Weekly
Owner
Finance + Founder
Why
>5% = mis-selling, bad onboarding, or poor delivery. <3% healthy.
